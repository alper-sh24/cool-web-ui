01 // The Philosophy

In an age of abstraction, we have forgotten the texture of the machine.

Complexity is often mistaken for progress. We layer frameworks upon frameworks until the original intent is buried under megabytes of runtime overhead.

I believe in Computing as Craft. The meticulous arrangement of bits, the surgical precision of memory management, and the brutalist beauty of the terminal.

Software should not just be "functional." It should be intentional. Every line of code is a weight we ask the processor to carry.

The goal is not to automate thought, but to clarify it. To build environments that reduce friction between the mind and the silicon.

Efficiency is an aesthetic. Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.
